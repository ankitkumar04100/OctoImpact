# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in OctoImpact a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Public or private attacks
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Reporting
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting **octopushackathon@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct. We may take action such as warning, temporary suspension, or banning contributors for any behavior that violates this code.
